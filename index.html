Student Echo is a dedicated service that enables verified students to provide and access insightful evaluations of courses and instructors at the University of Manitoba.

Vision Statement

Student Echo offers a dynamic forum for students to rate courses and instructors, fostering a space for community-driven academic improvement. Editable and interactive, it’s the pulse of student feedback.

Intended Users

Designed for the diverse voices of the University of Manitoba community, Student Echo serves not just students but academic stakeholders at large, with aspirations to connect multiple universities under its umbrella.
